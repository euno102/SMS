Why it matters

Unplanned outages cost time, money, and safety. We turn raw grid data into an early warning.

⚡

Real-time Monitoring

Continuously tracks power consumption and supply metrics across the grid to spot anomalies as they emerge.

📈

Predictive Analysis

Uses historical and live data patterns to forecast supply-demand imbalance before it becomes critical.

🔔

Early Alerts

Notifies operators the moment risk thresholds are crossed, enabling proactive load balancing.

How it works

A simple pipeline from raw grid data to actionable prevention.

1

Collect Data

Gather power consumption and supply data from sensors and grid endpoints.

2

Analyze Patterns

Model the balance between demand and supply to identify emerging risk.

3

Predict Risk

Flag areas approaching overload before an outage actually occurs.

4

Prevent Outage

Alert operators so load can be redistributed ahead of failure.
